Semantics

The new semantic tags allows us to build simpler layouts and clearer html code.

You can use the tags header, footer, section, article, nav and aside in your layouts to define different page areas:

Layout:
This is the header
Main navigation area
Side menu
An article
Another article
This is the footer
Code: 
<header>This is the header</header>
<nav>Main navigation area</nav>
<aside>Side menu</aside>
<section>
    <article>An article</article>
    <article>Another article</article>
</section>
<footer>Section footer</footer>

You can have several headers and footers in a page, but only one 'nav' and one 'aside'. Each section or article can have its own header and its own footer:

Layout:
Section header
Article header
An article
Article footer
Article header
Another article
Article footer
Section footer
Code: 
<section>
    <header>Section header</header>
    <article>
        <header>Article header</header>
            An article
        <footer>Article footer</footer>
    </article>
    <article>
        <header>Article header</header>
            Another article
        <footer>Article footer</footer>
    </article>
    <footer>Section footer</footer>
</section>

Video

Video element allows to show a video for any platform without any extra plugin.

Code:

    <video controls>
        <source src="pluto.mp4" type="video/mp4"/>
    </video>
